about us

In today's world of broken logistics links, we are a hub that connects commodity and logistics flows from different directions and places. This requires the cooperation of manufacturers, distributors and logistics operators to achieve the highest efficiency and lowest possible cost in each product with the requisite availability and flexibility.

The complexity of operations in international trade makes the support of a global logistics operator essential so that the trading company can focus on developing its competitive advantages.

why us

As licensed Customs brokers, Pontem company works with importers long before their cargo
is shipped.

Whether researching duty rates and admissibility requirements Pontem works with importers the whole length of their supply chains.

A Customs broker who knows the regulations and how to work with Customs, government agencies and freight forwarders, carriers, airlines and cargo handlers is essential for a business to succeed. We work with importers to structure their documents and transportation arrangements and ask the important questions in advance to minimize delays upon arrival.

If cargo is delayed, Pontem has the knowledge, the connections and the experience to solve the problem and get the cargo moving again with minimal delays and for the smallest additional cost as possible.
